HoloEN Connect the World Concert - Watchalong Event

What?

A livestream watchalong of the Hololive English Connect the World concert in a private cinema theater hall.

Where?

A privately rented cinema hall. The whole cinema will be closed except for this part and we will be able to cheer and have fun together. There will not be any food or drinks provided by the place! Further rules will be announced in the planning Discord server. GMaps location of the event.

When?

On the night between Sunday 2nd and Monday 3rd at 4am local time.

Doors open 3 AM

Concert starts 4 AM

Rules

  • Must buy your own streaming ticket to enter.
  • Checking of the streaming ticket / other requirements will be done before the concert.
  • We will split extra costs. This is a non-profit, fan-made event.

How do I join?

More information & entering the event in the planning Discord server.
